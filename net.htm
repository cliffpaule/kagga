About Kaga Green School

Nurturing Future Leaders Through Quality Education

Located in the heart of Kiambu County, Kaga Green School is a premier educational institution offering the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) from Grade 1 to Grade 9. Our school is committed to providing a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, creativity, and moral values.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a team of dedicated educators, we prepare our students to meet the challenges of the 21st century with confidence and competence.

Academic Excellence

Consistently ranked among the top schools in Kiambu County for academic performance.

Holistic Development

We focus on the intellectual, physical, emotional, and social development of each child.

Qualified Teachers

Our teaching staff are highly trained in CBC delivery and child-centered learning approaches.

Our CBC Approach

Learner-Centered

Our teaching methods focus on the individual needs and abilities of each student, ensuring personalized learning experiences.

Competency-Based

We emphasize the acquisition of knowledge, skills, values, and attitudes that prepare students for real-world challenges.

Innovative Teaching

Our teachers employ modern, interactive teaching methods that stimulate critical thinking and creativity.

Continuous Assessment

We monitor student progress through ongoing assessments that provide meaningful feedback for improvement.
